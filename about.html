<····· About me ·····>

I am Fibox, An illustrator and character designer. I've been drawing for 6+ years now (started when I was 20 years old). I specialize in 2D digital anime art. But I also enjoy traditional art and realistic fine art (mainly portrait and figure drawing). Below you can see some of the progress I've made over the years.

My biggest inspirations come from anime, manga and games. I especially enjoy the 'card' artworks from game companies. some that immediately come to my mind are the splash art from 'League of Legends' and the card art from 'Shadowverse'. Other than that, I also get a lot of motivation from watching freelance / social media artists online.

Another passion of mine is programming, and well... I'm proud to say this website is the first project I build on my own with HTML and CSS! I'm very much looking forward to upgrading this website every time I learn new skills.
